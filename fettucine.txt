Ingredients:
1 lb fettucine
1 lb hot itaian sausage
1 cup heavy cream
24 sage leaves
4 tablespoons olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
salt

simmer sausage covered in water for 10 minutes, set aside to cool
boil 4 quarts of water
heat oil in skillet, medium high.  fry sage until crisp, remove from pan
cut sausage into chunks, add to oil, brown.  add garlic when nearly done
remove sausage, drain fat from pan
Add cream, bring to a boil, scraping up browned bits, reduce
add sausage to cream
toss with fettucine
garnish with sage
serve
